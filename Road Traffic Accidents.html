About

I made this page to highlight the unfair representation of autonomous vehicles in the media. If you have any thoughts or feedback, please leave them in the comments below.

Human road traffic deaths are based on WHO figures from 2015. A search on BBC News and Google News found one case where a semi self-driving car was implicated in a car crash, and this was a very controversial case where the role of autonomous technology was disputed. A more recent case involved a pedestrian, who was struck by an autonomous Uber vehicle in Arizona.
